My beliefs

I'm obsessive about working and doing the best I possibly can. A 5 star rating is pass / fail for me - anything less is a fail, and I don't believe in failing. There are always solutions when talented people share the same goals.

Communication and setting expectations are keys to success. It can be difficult to communicate bad news but its better to get everyone on the same page and working together to solve problems. Silos are a thing of the past. Time is short, hesitation gets in the way. Gather information, come up with a plan, execute, and refine.

There is a rapidly expanding universe of amazing cloud based apps that improve the efficiency of business processes, productivity, and collaboration. The teams that learn to harness these will be unstopable. But not everyone is tech savy and the best tools are the ones that everyone is on board with. Minimize the number of tools you're using so you have a central point of truth.
